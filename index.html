Climate Change

By: Zainab, Lisa, Afsana, & Anosha

Welcome and we hope you enjoy playing our game “Climate Change”. And yes you might be thinking “gee what is this game about” and if you guessed Climate Change you are correct it is about Climate Change. Our game contains information regarding Climate Change and it allows you guys to learn more about it. By playing this game you are guaranteed to learn more about Climate Change and be more aware of the topic. We urge you to take charge in helping us spread awareness of this life threatening issue, and a plus side is that you get to play a fun and interesting trivia game and learn at the same time!
